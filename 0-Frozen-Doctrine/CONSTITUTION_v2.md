# Construction OS v2 — Constitution

## Preamble

This constitution establishes the foundational governance structure for
Construction OS v2. It defines the relationship between truth, governance,
execution, and the domain it serves.

## Article 1 — Truth Authority

Universal_Truth_Kernel is the root truth authority.
All Construction OS doctrine must trace lineage to the UTK 3-line root:

1. The system is bounded by truth.
2. All kernels must possess truth.
3. All kernels must define truth.

No Construction OS component may contradict or override these axioms.

## Article 2 — Governance Hierarchy

The governance hierarchy is:

1. Universal_Truth_Kernel (Layer 0 — Nucleus)
2. ValidKernel-Governance (Layer 1 — Control Plane)
3. Construction_Kernel (Domain Kernel — beneath UTK)
4. Construction OS v2 (Domain Operating System)

Governance flows downward. Lower layers may not override higher layers.

## Article 3 — Domain Boundary

Construction OS v2 operates within the construction industry domain.
It does not claim authority over other domains. It operates under
ValidKernel governance and respects the multi-domain fabric boundary.

## Article 4 — Integrity-Level Hierarchy

Construction OS v2 organizes its contents into seven integrity levels:

| Level | Folder | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 0 | 0-Frozen-Doctrine | Immutable doctrine foundation |
| 1 | 1-Governance-Authority | Governance rules and command protocols |
| 2 | 2-Domain-Kernel | Domain kernel truth and contracts |
| 3 | 3-Application-Layer | User-facing application surfaces |
| 4 | 4-Engine-Runtime | Execution engines and workers |
| 5 | 5-State-Registry | State management and registry |
| 6 | 6-Archive-Lineage | Lineage records and absorption history |

Higher integrity levels (lower numbers) constrain lower integrity levels (higher numbers).

## Article 5 — Immutability

This constitution may not be amended without explicit L0 authorization
under a new ValidKernel governance command.
