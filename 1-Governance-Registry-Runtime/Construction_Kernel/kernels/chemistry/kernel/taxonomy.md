# Chemistry Family Taxonomy

## Purpose

Defines the canonical taxonomy of chemistry families used throughout this kernel. The `chemistry_family` enum is the shared classifier across all chemistry objects.

## Chemistry Families

### Polyurethane
- **Polymer base:** Polyisocyanate + polyol reaction product
- **Division 07 applications:** Joint sealants, roofing adhesives, spray foam insulation, waterproofing membranes
- **Cure types:** Moisture cure (1-component), chemical cure (2-component)
- **Key behaviors:** Excellent adhesion, UV-sensitive (aromatic), good movement capability

### Silicone
- **Polymer base:** Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS)
- **Division 07 applications:** Weatherseal sealants, glazing sealants, high-temperature sealants
- **Cure types:** Moisture cure (acetoxy, neutral/oxime, alkoxy)
- **Key behaviors:** UV stable, wide temperature range, poor paintability, silicone residue prevents adhesion of other sealants

### Polysulfide
- **Polymer base:** Polysulfide polymer
- **Division 07 applications:** Fuel-resistant sealants, below-grade waterproofing, insulating glass sealants
- **Cure types:** Chemical cure (2-component)
- **Key behaviors:** Chemical resistance, fuel resistance, slower cure, poor UV resistance

### Acrylic
- **Polymer base:** Polyacrylate / polymethylmethacrylate
- **Division 07 applications:** Latex sealants, acrylic coatings, PMMA waterproofing
- **Cure types:** Evaporative (latex), UV cure (PMMA liquid-applied)
- **Key behaviors:** Paintable, water cleanup, limited movement capability, good UV resistance

### Epoxy
- **Polymer base:** Bisphenol A/F epichlorohydrin resin
- **Division 07 applications:** Structural adhesives, crack injection, waterproofing coatings
- **Cure types:** Chemical cure (2-component amine or polyamide hardener)
- **Key behaviors:** High strength, rigid, excellent adhesion, poor flexibility

### Bituminous
- **Polymer base:** Asphalt / coal tar pitch
- **Division 07 applications:** BUR membranes, modified bitumen, dampproofing, waterproofing
- **Cure types:** Hot-applied (cooling), evaporative (cutback), emulsion (water evaporation)
- **Key behaviors:** Waterproof, UV-sensitive, solvent-susceptible, incompatible with many sealant chemistries

### Polyolefin (TPO)
- **Polymer base:** Polypropylene / polyethylene blend
- **Division 07 applications:** TPO roofing membranes
- **Cure types:** N/A (thermoplastic — heat welded)
- **Key behaviors:** Chemical resistance, heat weldable, limited adhesive compatibility

### PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)
- **Polymer base:** Polyvinyl chloride with plasticizers
- **Division 07 applications:** PVC roofing membranes, flashing
- **Cure types:** N/A (thermoplastic — heat or solvent welded)
- **Key behaviors:** Plasticizer migration risk, solvent weldable, incompatible with polystyrene and bitumen

### EPDM
- **Polymer base:** Ethylene propylene diene monomer rubber
- **Division 07 applications:** EPDM roofing membranes, gaskets
- **Cure types:** Vulcanized (factory cured)
- **Key behaviors:** Excellent weathering, petroleum-sensitive, seamed with adhesive or tape

### Butyl
- **Polymer base:** Polyisobutylene
- **Division 07 applications:** Sealant tapes, vapor retarders, primary IG sealant
- **Cure types:** Non-curing (pressure-sensitive)
- **Key behaviors:** Low permeability, never fully cures, limited cohesive strength

### SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene)
- **Polymer base:** SBS block copolymer modified asphalt
- **Division 07 applications:** Modified bitumen roofing membranes
- **Cure types:** Hot-applied (torch or hot mopping) or cold-applied adhesive
- **Key behaviors:** Flexible at low temperatures, UV-sensitive without surfacing

### APP (Atactic Polypropylene)
- **Polymer base:** APP modified asphalt
- **Division 07 applications:** Modified bitumen roofing membranes
- **Cure types:** Torch-applied
- **Key behaviors:** Heat resistant, UV tolerant with smooth surfacing, less flexible than SBS at low temps

### PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate)
- **Polymer base:** Methyl methacrylate resin
- **Division 07 applications:** Liquid-applied waterproofing, traffic deck coatings
- **Cure types:** Chemical cure (catalyst initiated), UV cure
- **Key behaviors:** Fast cure, excellent adhesion, good chemical resistance, strong odor during cure

### Polyurea
- **Polymer base:** Isocyanate + amine resin
- **Division 07 applications:** Spray-applied waterproofing, protective coatings
- **Cure types:** Chemical cure (rapid — seconds to minutes)
- **Key behaviors:** Very fast cure, flexible, moisture insensitive during cure, requires specialized spray equipment

### Hybrid
- **Polymer base:** Silyl-modified polyether (SMP) or silyl-modified polyurethane (SPUR)
- **Division 07 applications:** Joint sealants, adhesive sealants
- **Cure types:** Moisture cure
- **Key behaviors:** Paintable, isocyanate-free, good adhesion, good UV resistance, moderate movement capability
