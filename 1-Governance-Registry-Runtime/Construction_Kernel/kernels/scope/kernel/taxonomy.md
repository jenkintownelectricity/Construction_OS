# Scope Taxonomy

## Purpose

Defines the classification categories for scope within CSI Division 07 -- Building Envelope Systems. This taxonomy organizes scope records into domain-specific categories.

## Primary Scope Categories

### 1. Roofing (07 50 00)
Membrane roofing systems including TPO, PVC, EPDM, and modified bitumen. Encompasses field membrane, insulation, cover boards, and roof accessories.

### 2. Waterproofing (07 10 00)
Below-grade and above-grade waterproofing systems. Includes foundation waterproofing, plaza deck waterproofing, and dampproofing.

### 3. Air Barriers (07 27 00)
Air barrier systems for building envelope continuity. Includes fluid-applied, self-adhered, and mechanically fastened systems. Closely coordinated with weather-resistive barriers (07 25 00).

### 4. Thermal Insulation (07 21 00)
Continuous insulation and cavity insulation as part of the building envelope. Board stock, spray-applied, and blown-in insulation within envelope assemblies.

### 5. Flashing (07 60 00)
Through-wall flashing, transition flashing, and termination flashing. Critical interface element connecting multiple building systems.

### 6. Sheet Metal (07 60 00)
Copings, counter-flashing, gutters, downspouts, and custom sheet metal fabrications. Closely coordinated with roofing and masonry trades.

### 7. Sealants (07 92 00)
Joint sealant systems including perimeter sealants, expansion joint sealants, and weatherseal applications. Part of the broader joint protection category (07 90 00).

### 8. Fireproofing (07 81 00, 07 84 00)
Spray-applied fireproofing and firestopping at penetrations and joints. Includes intumescent and cementitious fireproofing systems.
