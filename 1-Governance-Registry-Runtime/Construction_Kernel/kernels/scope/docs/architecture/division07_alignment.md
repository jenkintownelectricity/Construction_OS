# Division 07 Alignment

## Purpose

This document defines how the Scope Kernel maps scope objects to CSI Division 07 -- Building Envelope Systems.

## Scope Categories by Subdivision

### 07 10 00 -- Dampproofing and Waterproofing
- Below-grade waterproofing scope
- Plaza deck waterproofing scope
- Foundation dampproofing scope
- Typical trades: waterproofing installer, concrete contractor (substrate)
- Key interfaces: structural concrete, drainage systems, protection board

### 07 20 00 -- Thermal Protection
- Continuous insulation scope
- Cavity insulation scope (when part of envelope assembly)
- Typical trades: insulation installer
- Key interfaces: air barrier, cladding support, structural framing

### 07 25 00 / 07 27 00 -- Weather and Air Barriers
- Air barrier system scope (fluid-applied, self-adhered, mechanically fastened)
- Weather-resistive barrier scope
- Typical trades: air barrier installer
- Key interfaces: window/door openings, roof-to-wall transitions, penetrations

### 07 50 00 -- Membrane Roofing
- TPO, PVC, EPDM, modified bitumen roofing scope
- Roof insulation and cover board scope
- Typical trades: roofing contractor
- Key interfaces: sheet metal, penetrations, roof-to-wall, parapets, drains

### 07 60 00 -- Flashing and Sheet Metal
- Through-wall flashing scope
- Counter-flashing and cap flashing scope
- Copings, gutters, downspouts scope
- Typical trades: sheet metal contractor
- Key interfaces: roofing membrane, masonry, curtain wall

### 07 81 00 / 07 84 00 -- Fireproofing and Firestopping
- Spray-applied fireproofing scope
- Firestopping at penetrations and joints scope
- Typical trades: fireproofing contractor
- Key interfaces: structural steel, MEP penetrations, rated assemblies

### 07 90 00 -- Joint Protection
- Sealant joint scope
- Expansion joint scope
- Typical trades: sealant applicator
- Key interfaces: curtain wall, precast panels, window perimeters

## Cross-Division Dependencies

Division 07 scope frequently interfaces with:
- Division 03 (Concrete) -- substrates for waterproofing and air barriers
- Division 04 (Masonry) -- through-wall flashing, weeps, cavity drainage
- Division 05 (Metals) -- structural steel for fireproofing, cladding support
- Division 08 (Openings) -- window and door integration with air/water barriers
- Division 22/23 (Mechanical) -- roof penetrations, equipment curbs
