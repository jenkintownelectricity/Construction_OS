# Detail Atlas — Construction Intelligence Platform

## The Industry Problem

Construction details — the technical drawings that show how building systems connect, terminate, and transition — are still produced manually. An architect or detailer draws each condition by hand in CAD, one at a time. When a manufacturer changes a product, every detail that uses it must be redrawn. When a new project starts, the same 10 conditions get drawn from scratch again.

This is slow, expensive, error-prone, and doesn't scale.

## What Detail Atlas Does

Detail Atlas is a parametric construction intelligence engine. Instead of drawing details one at a time, it generates them from structured manufacturer data and measured geometry rules.

**Input:** Select a manufacturer system (e.g., Barrett PMMA) and a construction condition (e.g., equipment curb, pipe penetration, parapet wall).

**Output:** A print-ready detail sheet with accurate section geometry, correct layer stacks, proper dimensions, callout keys, and manufacturer-specific product references — generated in seconds, not hours.

## Why This Is Different From CAD Drafting

- **Parametric:** Change a dimension (curb height, insulation thickness) and the detail regenerates automatically
- **Manufacturer-aware:** Each detail knows which products go where, in what order, with what constraints
- **Multi-format:** Same geometry exports as SVG (screen/print), PDF (client delivery), and DXF (CAD overlay)
- **Governed:** Every detail traces back to a calibration specimen and assembly DNA template — no orphan drawings

## Why Manufacturers Care

- Their details are always generated correctly with their products in the right positions
- New conditions can be added without redrawing from scratch
- Product changes propagate to all details that use them
- They can offer parametric detail packages to architects and contractors as a value-add

## Why Architects and Contractors Care

- Details that match the actual spec'd products, not generic placeholders
- Print-ready sheets that don't need manual cleanup
- DXF files that drop into their CAD environment with proper layers
- Consistent quality across all conditions in a project
